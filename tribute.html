Jacob Collier

Jacob Collier is a music prodigy of the contemporary times. He is a singer, arranger, composer, producer, and multi-instrumentalist, he's sort of an DIY philosopher of the music. Born in the UK, he started to upload videos covering and arranging popular songs like "Flinstones" and "Don't you worry about a thing". His style is unique, mixing jazz theory (often an extreme use of reharmonization), with classical and popular elements that results in an interesting fusion that worth listening once in your life at least.
